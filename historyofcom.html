Abacus

The abacus is the earliest known calculating device. It was used in ancient China and other parts of the world.

ENIAC

The ENIAC was the first electronic general-purpose computer. It was built in the United States in 1945.

UNIVAC

The UNIVAC was the first commercial computer in the United States. It was designed by J. Presper Eckert and John Mauchly and built by the Remington Rand company in the 1950s.

IBM System/360

The IBM System/360 was a family of mainframe computers that was introduced in 1964. It was widely used in businesses and government organizations for many years.

Apple II

The Apple II was one of the first successful personal computers. It was introduced by Apple Computer in 1977 and was widely used in homes and schools.

IBM PC

The IBM PC was the first personal computer to gain widespread popularity. It was introduced by IBM in 1981 and used the MS-DOS operating system.
